What Are My Favorite Hobbies?



Volleyball

I started playing volleyball when I was about 10 years old.
I played throughout my middle school and high school years as a full-time setter.
Not only did I play in school, but I played it outside with my friends.
We would go to the park every day after school and played.My role switched
from a setter to a defensive player. I traveled to Minnesota, Georgia, and
South Carolina to play in competitive tournaments. Most of the time, I played
volleyball outdoors and had an annual sports tournament with the Hmong community.



Photography

Growing up, I always watched America's Next Top Model. I was intrigued
with the makeup, glam, clothes, and being able to create interesting photos.
That's when I opened up a whole new world of loving photography. I love creating
images that make you feel something like a kid who looks at their idol. I wanted
to create that same feeling of extreme bliss when you look at an image. Not only
did I take photos of landscaping, I also tried out modeling. Though taking photos
in my own backyard didn't count, I loved the idea of dressing up and creating a
cool photo in hopes that one day a little girl or boy would look up to and want to
become me. Photography will always be a passion of mine, though I hardly pick up a camera anymore.



Special Effects Makeup

Along with photography, I loved creating horror and gore theme makeup.
The idea of being able to create something that looks realistic though isn't
intrigues me. I create different looks that could either be mystical, gore,
pretty, look-alikes, animals, zombies... you name it! I enjoy the reaction I
get from people who enjoy my looks and being told that I could turn it into
a job. I didn't continue this as a career, but is definitely a passion I still
hold onto till this day.



Traveling

Throughout my life, I've always wanted to travel. To explore the world
and to be able to see different cultures. Myself coming from a different
culture, I love seeing how it resembles or differs from mine. I have yet to
travel out of the country but it is definitely one of my goals in life to
travel to most of the possible countries. I have gone to many beautiful beaches
here in the US and love traveling to different hiking spots with amazing views.
It's crazy how everybody speaks a different language, but can all come together and
enjoy each other's different culture.



Family

My family is very family-oriented. Sometimes I think a
little too much. But I love enjoying each and everyone's
company and coming together and laughing and enjoying time
together. Time is always fleeting, and I choose to enjoy it with
my loved ones. We all grew up together and are very close knit
and now that everybody is grown up and going their own path and living
their own lives, it is definitely a lot harder to be together.
Mostly, I love having the family out together to enjoy some time in the
outdoors whether it is hiking, swimming, picnicing. Family is and will
always be super important to me.
